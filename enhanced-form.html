Introduction to Interior Design

Interior design basics

Interior design is guided by elements.
These elements guide interior designers as biulding blocks
for creating functional aesthetically pleasing, and harmonious spaces.

There are seven elements in interior design

The Elements include;

  1. space
  2. Line
  3. Form
  4. Light
  5. colour
  6. Texture
  7. Pattern

Definition of form
Form

Form refers to the three-dimensional shape of objects
such as the shape of a room,
furniture, or decorative items.

Texture

Texture refers to the surface quality of materials,
both visual and tactile.
It adds depth, interest, and
dimension to a space.
